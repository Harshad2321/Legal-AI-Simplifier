RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement is made between Sarah Johnson ("Landlord") and [TENANT NAME] ("Tenant") for the premises located at 123 Oak Street, Apartment 2B, Springfield, IL 62701 ("Premises").

1. TERM
The lease term shall be for twelve (12) months, beginning on [START DATE] and ending on [END DATE].

2. RENT
Monthly rent is $1,200, due on the first day of each month. Late fees of $50 will be charged for payments received after the 5th day of the month.

3. SECURITY DEPOSIT
Tenant shall pay a security deposit of $1,800 prior to occupancy. This deposit may be applied to unpaid rent or damages beyond normal wear and tear.

4. USE OF PREMISES
The Premises shall be used solely as a private residence for Tenant and immediate family members. No pets are allowed without written consent.

5. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
Landlord shall maintain the Premises in habitable condition. Tenant is responsible for minor repairs and maintenance under $100.

6. UTILITIES
Tenant is responsible for electricity, gas, water, internet, and cable. Landlord pays for trash collection and building maintenance.

7. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this lease with 30 days written notice. Early termination by Tenant may result in forfeiture of security deposit.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This lease shall be governed by Illinois state law and local housing regulations.
