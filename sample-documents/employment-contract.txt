EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [DATE], between TechCorp Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Company"), and [EMPLOYEE NAME] ("Employee").

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
Employee shall serve as Software Engineer and shall perform such duties as may be assigned by the Company. Employee agrees to devote full-time attention to the business of the Company.

2. COMPENSATION
a) Base Salary: Employee shall receive an annual base salary of $85,000, payable in accordance with Company's regular payroll practices.
b) Benefits: Employee shall be eligible to participate in Company's benefit plans as they may exist from time to time.

3. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without cause, upon thirty (30) days written notice.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee acknowledges that during employment, Employee may have access to certain confidential information of the Company. Employee agrees to maintain the confidentiality of such information.

5. NON-COMPETE
During employment and for a period of twelve (12) months after termination, Employee shall not engage in any business that competes with the Company within a 50-mile radius of Company's headquarters.

6. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
